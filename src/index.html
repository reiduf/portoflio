About Me

Hey there! I'm Reina, an endlessly curious and creatively-minded programmer with a billion and one interests.

My background is all about visuals: I earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Graphic Communication with a minor in Packaging. For about seven years, I worked in the print industry, managing diverse projects and designing packages.

During the COVID pandemic, my free time transformed into a playful exploration of online JavaScript tutorials and "Coding for Dummies" books. I found the challenge exceptionally rewarding, shifting from the visual to the logical hemisphere of my brain.

Realizing programming seamlessly combines my past and future, I completed a 12-week Software Engineering Bootcamp with General Assembly. Now, having graduated, I'm immersed in daily learning and networking, eager to blend my creative background with newfound technical prowess!
