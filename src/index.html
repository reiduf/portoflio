About Me

Hey there! I'm Reina, an endlessly curious and creatively-minded programmer with a billion and one interests.

My background is all about visuals: I earned a Bachelor of Science in Graphic Communication with a minor in Packaging back in 2016. For about seven years, I had the absolute pleasure of working for a print company, managing and creating projects that spanned a wide range of clients and industries.

I adored the daily variety, jumping from projects related to cannabis, beauty, medical, pharmaceuticals, and everything in between. Fascinating stuff, but I found myself itching for a change, craving technical challenges, skill-building, and contact with industries beyond print.

During the COVID pandemic, my free time transformed into a playful exploration of JavaScript tutorials online and an entertaining dive into "Coding for Dummies" books. I found the challenge exceptionally rewarding, experiencing a peculiar shift from the visual to the logical hemisphere of my brain.

Realizing that programming combined the best of my old career with everything I wanted in a new one, I decided to take the plunge. I recently wrapped up a 12-week intensive Software Engineering Bootcamp with General Assembly. Now, I'm immersed in learning and networking with industry professionals every day, eagerly seeking an opportunity to blend my creative background with my newfound technical prowess!
